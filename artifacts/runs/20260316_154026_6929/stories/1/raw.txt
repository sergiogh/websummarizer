<p><img fetchpriority="high" decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-27337 aligncenter lazy" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20300%20225'%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://www.qureca.com/storage/2026/03/QURECA_QCentroid_QBase_2@2x-100-300x225.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="225" data-srcset="https://www.qureca.com/storage/2026/03/QURECA_QCentroid_QBase_2@2x-100-300x225.jpg 300w, https://www.qureca.com/storage/2026/03/QURECA_QCentroid_QBase_2@2x-100-1024x768.jpg 1024w" data-sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" /></p>
<p>The training will be delivered by QURECA, the global leader in quantum education and workforce development. Sessions will take place at QBase, a dedicated quantum applications hub in the City of London, in partnership with Northeastern University London. QBase is founded by the team behind Level39, Europe&#8217;s most successful fintech hub, and Tech Nation. QBase is a physical hub and ecosystem &#8211; a curated environment where businesses can begin exploring quantum methods today in readiness for quantum advantage tomorrow. The programme is aimed at executives and investors across finance, energy, defence, government, healthcare, and other key sectors.</p>
<p>The training will take the form of a comprehensive four-hour in-person workshop at QBase, giving attendees a clear picture of quantum&#8217;s potential impact in their sector and the steps they need to take to get ahead. Participants will learn how to explore sector-specific use cases and develop proof-of-concept experiments. The real-world application  work will be enabled by QCentroid’s QuantumOps platform, which provides a practical environment for building and testing quantum use cases without the need for deep technical skills and allows companies to refine and extend their experiments after the workshop.</p>
<p>“QURECA has built its reputation as the go-to partner for quantum education globally. We’re excited that this partnership will help us extend that reach to some of the most influential decision-makers in the UK economy.” &#8211; <strong>Dr Araceli Venegas-Gomez, CEO and Founder of QURECA</strong>.</p>
<p>&#8220;Quantum is no longer a distant prospect. This is an exciting opportunity to reach a broader audience of decision-makers who will ultimately shape how quantum technologies are adopted across industries&#8221; <strong>– Eric Van der Kleij, Partner, EdenBase</strong></p>
<p>“Quantum only matters when it moves from slides to real workflows,” said  <strong>Carlos Kuchkovsky, CEO and Founder at QCentroid</strong>. “By powering the hands‑on elements of this programme, our platform helps take the workshop experience to another level and lets executives turn theory into repeatable practice both in the room and back inside their organisations.”</p>
<p>Executives and investors interested in the programme can contact QBase directly at: <a href="/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#d4adb5a7b9bdba94b1b0b1bab6b5a7b1fab7bbb9"><span class="__cf_email__" data-cfemail="8cf5edffe1e5e2cce9e8e9e2eeedffe9a2efe3e1">[email&#160;protected]</span></a></p>
<p><strong>About QURECA</strong> QURECA provides professional services, resources, and training to drive the growth of the global quantum technologies ecosystem. We bring together industry, academia, and government by creating education and training opportunities, delivering tailored workshops, and organising events that foster collaboration and innovation. Through outreach and consulting, we support organisations in navigating the quantum landscape and help make quantum technologies accessible, engaging, and impactful. Our mission is to build a strong, connected, and inclusive quantum community worldwide.</p>
<p>For more information, visit <a href="https://www.qureca.com/">www.qureca.com</a></p>
<p><strong>About EdenBase</strong> EdenBase is a venture capital fund and ecosystem builder (QBase) focused on frontier technologies. Founded by entrepreneurs and investors behind Level39 and Tech Nation, EdenBase backs the companies defining tomorrow and builds the ecosystem to support them. QBase is a quantum ecosystem and physical hub in London, in partnership with Northeastern University London. It brings together quantum start-ups, investors, corporates, policymakers in a shared environment to support collaboration, learning and experimentation; accelerating the translation of quantum potential into real-world adoption.</p>
<p>For more information, visit <a href="https://www.edenbase.com/">www.edenbase.com</a>.</p>
<p><strong>About QCentroid</strong> QCentroid is a software platform that enables enterprise adoption of quantum computing at scale, from experimentation to production deployment. Its agentic QuantumOps platform provides a practical environment for organisations to build, validate, and deploy quantum solvers for real business use cases — without friction and without the need for deep technical expertise. QCentroid serves organisations in several sectors, including finance, energy, logistics, pharma, and more.</p>
