Why it matters: The study shows potential to deliver solutions on par with classical methods for complex financial optimization, with robust performance despite hardware noise. Key numbers/people: Published 29 September 2025 by Roberto Lo Nardo and Gabriele Agliardi, with contributions including Brian Jaffe, Bimal Mehta, and Paul Malloy. MAS and Partners Unveil QKD Sandbox Technical Report: Quantum Security in Financial Services

What happened: Monetary Authority of Singapore, DBS, HSBC, OCBC, UOB, SPTel, and SpeQtral released a technical report on a financial-sector Quantum Key Distribution sandbox, a proof-of-concept launched under an August 2024 Memorandum of Understanding. The layered setup (QKD, Key Management System, Secure Application Entity) ran over SPTel fiber and National Quantum-Safe Network Plus trusted nodes, using ETSI GS QKD 014; tests spanned two phases from late 2024 to early 2025. Why it matters: Trials confirmed QKD strengthened communication-link security and integrated with banking systems in a controlled, realistic environment. Key numbers/people: Four banks; at any time, MAS and two banks connected; MAS issued quantum-risk guidance in February 2024 and ran a 2024 cross-border trial with Banque de France. Direct impact: Report urges interoperability standards, smoother integration layers, and tamper-resistant, auditable trusted nodes—marking a significant step in practical quantum-safe cybersecurity.
